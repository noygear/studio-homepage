Thesis · Economics 101

Value of a currency = transactions ÷ units outstanding.

From that first principle, Noygear backs companies that bring real economic activity on-chain when it makes sense — and stays away from speculation, token games, and memetic flywheels.

V = Σ Transactions / Units Outstanding

Every venture we build carries a second non-negotiable: upskilling for everyone in the loop — users, vendors, and our own pods. Compounding human capital is the only moat we trust.

01 — Real

Real-world transactions

Work orders, clinical encounters, training hours — not tokens pointing at other tokens.

02 — Upskilled

Every user gets more capable

Every venture ships measurable capability gains for users, vendors, and the studio itself.

03 — Durable

Operator-owned

Software operators choose — not software sold around them or on top of them.
