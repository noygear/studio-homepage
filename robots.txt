# robots.txt for noygear.ai
#
# Search crawlers may fetch every page, including the legal pages. The legal
# pages (terms.html, privacy.html) carry a noindex meta tag, so allowing the
# fetch lets search engines read that tag and keep the pages out of results.
# A blanket Disallow would block the fetch, the noindex would never be seen,
# and the URLs could still surface as bare, link-only results.
#
# AI and LLM crawlers and dataset collectors are disallowed from the legal
# pages, because they rely on robots.txt rather than on the noindex tag.
# Note: robots.txt is advisory. Well-behaved crawlers honor it, not all do.

Disallow:
